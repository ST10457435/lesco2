Code of Conduct

At Glenmark Christian School, we uphold a high standard of behavior that reflects our commitment to excellence, respect, and integrity. Our Code of Conduct is designed to ensure a safe, supportive, and productive environment for students, staff, and visitors alike.

Our Principles

  • Respect: We treat everyone with dignity and value diverse perspectives.
  • Integrity: We act honestly and ethically in all our endeavors.
  • Responsibility: We take ownership of our actions and contribute positively to our community.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in both academic and personal pursuits.

Expectations

Every member of our school community is expected to:

  • Adhere to school policies and procedures
  • Maintain a respectful and inclusive attitude towards others
  • Engage in honest and ethical behavior both inside and outside the classroom
  • Contribute to a positive learning environment by supporting peers and staff
  • Accept responsibility for personal actions and learn from mistakes

Enforcement

Our school administration is committed to enforcing these standards fairly and consistently. Violations of the Code of Conduct will be addressed promptly through a clear and respectful process designed to restore trust and uphold our community values.

By adhering to this Code of Conduct, you help us create an environment where everyone can thrive and achieve their full potential.
